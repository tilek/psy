Six basic needs

1. Safety
   Stable family. Physically and emotionally predictable parents.
   No physical abuse, abandonement, death.
2. Reliable attachment
3. Autonomy
4. Adequate self-esteem, competency
5. Free expression of emotions and needs, spontaneity, play
6. Realistic boundaries and self-control

Source: Reinventing Your Life, J.E. Young, J.S. Klosko
Translation to Russian: https://knowledge.org.ua/ru/shest-bazovyh-potrebnostej-detstva/
